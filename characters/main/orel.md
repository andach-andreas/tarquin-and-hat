# O'Rel Varys

O'Rel is a half-dragon bard who eventually bonds with Memory itself, allowing the story to be written. He is a researcher and librarian in Pangenia and is passionate about discovering the secrets of the universe. 

He grew up in a small fishing village on a long-forgotten planet. His father died on a boat when he was young, and he was raised by the men of that village. 

When he was a teenager, he went off and got lost in the nearby woods, taking overnight shelter in a cave in which a hermit lived. The hermit turned out to be an ancient dragon, a sentient species from a different universe who were taken. 

MUCH later in the story, it's revealed that the hermit is O'Rel's father, making him half-dragon, and explaining some of his strange magical powers. 

O'rel eventually binds with Memory, creating a link between his mortal form, the Primordial and the plane of Harmonium itself.
