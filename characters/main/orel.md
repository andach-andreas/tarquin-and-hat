# O'Rel Varys

O'Rel is a demihuman bard who eventually bonds with Memory itself, allowing the story to be written. He is a researcher and librarian in Pangenia and is passionate about discovering the secrets of the universe. 

He grew up in a small fishing village on a long-forgotten planet. His father died on a boat when he was young, and he was raised by the men of that village.
