# The Resistance

When Will detects Destruction's use of his power in Skepsyris, he jumps into action, and starts influencing powerful magical groups in Pangenia to investigate. 

Will, by his nature, is able to interfere more directly in mortal lives, though he still does so very sparingly. 

The group clash with a number of known liches and determine that Karzarios is making a power play, though have no idea about Destruction. Concerned about this, they form a loosely-organised group that they just refer to as 'The Resistance'. 

The group allows itself to be led by Nin Mosbeam, a ludicrously powerful wizard from Micah's planet. 

## Clash on Terra

The Resistance receive information that the Unity Coalition are planning on attacking Terra en masse to demoralise and force Nin into battle directly. They act quickly, building thousands of defensive structures that later become known as the vaults on this planet, to store information (in case they lose) and to act as stores for weapons, arcane gems and food for soldiers. 

Nin returns to Terra to inform the people and lead the fight. They follow him willingly, as he's viewed as a demigod by most mortals. 

The resistance arrive and the fight begins, causing massive damage and killing almost all the population.
