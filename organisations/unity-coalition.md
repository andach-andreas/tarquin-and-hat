# Unity Coalition

The Unity Coalition is a collection of beings led by Karzairos, the primordial in charge of Skepsyris. 

A few billion years after the creation of the Universe, Destruction finds out about it, and decides to interfere for his own benefit. He could easily have reduced the entire universe to dust, but is concerned that would play his hand too early and decides on caution. Specifically, he wants to see if the free will of the mortals could be used for his own benefit. 

He visits the universe and the conceptual planes, scouting them out, and decides to focus on Skepsyris. The concepts of Chaos and Tension play into his desires, and he considered Dramalia, but ultimately required the concept of Truth more than Illusion. 

Destruction takes up the guise of a mage warrior initially. He can't cast normal magic, that being the domain of Will, but is able to effectively create his own school of magic, and as magical research is common among mortals, this isn't weird by itself. The Celestials jockey constantly for power and Destruction is able to use this to endear himself to Karzairos by winning increasingly impossile victories for him as a general in his army. 

Eventually Karzarios grows suspicious that this man might grow more powerful than him, and summons his best warriors to subdue and kill Destruction. Of course, they fail completely, and Destruction returns to Karzairos entirely unharmed, with one of them left un-disintegrated so that he can show off his power directly. 

Destruction effectively reduces the man to antimatter and creates an explosion that would decimate a solar system. The plane of Skepsyris is scarred brutally by this explosion, which is felt across Pangenia but ultimately nobody, not even Pan herself, knows what it is. Will does though, deduces what Destruction is doing, and starts work on a resistance immediately. 

## The Transfer

Destruction offers Karzarios this power, and tells him that if he masters it, he can control the whole universe and userp Pan. He claims that the power is too strong for a mortal, and is destroying him, so he wishes to give it up to be at peace. He explains that Karzarios is unfamiliar with the power, and won't be able to use it as well as him for a long time, but nonetheless, he gives up a piece of his Primordial nature willingly. 

This manifests as a glowing orb, about the size of a golf ball. Perfectly smooth, perfectly still until unbound. When Karzarios touches it, he is able to use a tiny fraction of it, but every time he uses it, it casuses extreme fatigue, even to a Celestial. Destruction fades away, "thanking" Karzarios for releasing him from his torment. 

Karzarios practices with his power over time, and Destruction retires to observe. 

## The Uprising

Quickly, Karzarios gains followers. He invites liches and other such entities in to gain their loyalty and have them swear fealty. 

Over a few thousand years, his followers call themselves the Unity Coalition, an almost deliberately ironic name to symbolise their desire to bring the entire universe under one rule. Of course, they fight between themselves constantly, but no-one can counter the raw power of Destruction, so Karzarios himself is never challenged - even the most powerful lich is nothing against it. 

They bring a variety of worlds under their control, obviously starting with the ones with a strong connection to Skepsyris itself. 

They end up taking over Alethorragia and using the polyphotons to further synchronise their efforts, eventually mounting an assault on Pangenia itself. 

## The Assault

The Resistance fight the Unity Coalition across Pangenia and are successfully fought back. 

In the decisive final fight, Nin goads Karzarios to use the power of Destruction, then attacks him when he's weak, destroying him and forcing him to reform in Skepsyris. 

## Current State

Karzarios takes thousands of years to recover, and is in a highly damaged state by Tarquin's time. Various liches, etc. remain powerful and try to hunt down Karzarios's mysterious power, the nature of which remains completely unknown.
