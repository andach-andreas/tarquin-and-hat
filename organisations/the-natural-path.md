# The Natural Path

There is an established Church that is a front for control and the collection of knowledge. It has an astonishing collection of crystals – though few know the full extent of it. It also has scientists working on its behalf to collect information privately.

They recently developed the ability to triangulate the sources of magical power, which in turn gives them the ability to predict vault locations. It is well-known amongst spellcasters that magic dust can be very slightly attracted to spellcasting and sources of power – if you leave some dust and a gem on a desk overnight, the dust will move closer to the gem. But it’s very slow and completely impractical for all possible purposes.

The church reverse-engineered a device that was found in a vault to find that by arranging crystals in a tetrahedron this would happen far quicker and locate magic much further away. They send researchers to walk the land and capture the magical forces at various locations to triangulate sources, then sell these locations to vault hunters.

The church is looking to extend its control at all possible times and has recently taken to a political campaign of trying to persuade, threaten and bribe local leaders to its cause, hoping that the ordinary people will follow. This has  been generally successful and is resulting in a significant increase in church followship. About 20% of people are devout church followers, 20% of people are in favour of it, and 60% of people don’t care.

## Teachings

The church’s teachings are fundamentally that nature is good, and people should be focusing on that, and agriculture, and certainly not magic.

Magic is acceptable in the church’s hands, or when it directly helps nature or agriculture (which is convenient, because it almost never does) but otherwise is heretical.

## Commandments

1. Nature blesses the faithful; till the land and it shall reward you. 
2. The Church is your shield against chaos; trust in its light.
3. Love life, create life, and honour the sacred bond of man and woman.
4. Shun paths that turn away from life’s fertile design.
5. Magic, when unbridled, brings sorrow; in the Church’s hand, it brings bounty.
6. To hoard magic is to starve the world; share it with the Church.
7. The Crystals sing the Earth’s secret song; protect them.
8. Speak openly of any who misuse magic, that healing may begin.
9. Uphold leaders who walk with the Church; they walk with life itself.
10. Remember: loyalty to Earth, to life, and to the Church are one.
