# Euales (Εὐαλής) - The Harmonium

*   **Concepts:** Order + Collaboration + Truth
*   **Celestial:** **Althenos**, the Architect of Accord. A serene, six-eyed being who speaks in axioms that reshape the land.
*   **Physical Description:** An endless expanse of crystalline geometric islands floating in a silver void, reminiscent of the Astral Plane but rigidly structured. The "air" hums with a perfect, resonant chord. Gravity is subjective but always orients towards the nearest surface. Light does not cast shadows here; it permeates everything.
*   **Hazards:** **Crystallization**. The plane seeks to correct "flaws" in organic beings. Visitors who stay too long begin to turn into crystal statues, their chaotic thoughts replaced by perfect, static axioms.
*   **Etymology:** Derived from "Εὖ" (well, good) + "Ἀλήθεια" (truth) – “Well-Truth” or "Harmonious Truth".
