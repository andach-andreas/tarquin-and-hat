# Conceptual Planes

The Conceptual Planes are realms formed from the resonance of the fundamental concepts created by Pan. Each plane is ruled by a Celestial and presents unique physical characteristics and hazards to travelers, drawing inspiration from the metaphysical realms of the multiverse.

Over time, each plane bled into nearby multiverses (which did not have the idea of will) and pulled over matter which took on Will of its own, becoming living creatures by themselves, which obeyed different rules to normal living things. 

## Euales (Εὐαλής) - The Harmonium
*   **Concepts:** Order + Collaboration + Truth
*   **Celestial:** **Althenos**, the Architect of Accord. A serene, six-eyed being who speaks in axioms that reshape the land.
*   **Physical Description:** An endless expanse of crystalline geometric islands floating in a silver void, reminiscent of the Astral Plane but rigidly structured. The "air" hums with a perfect, resonant chord. Gravity is subjective but always orients towards the nearest surface. Light does not cast shadows here; it permeates everything.
*   **Hazards:** **Crystallization**. The plane seeks to correct "flaws" in organic beings. Visitors who stay too long begin to turn into crystal statues, their chaotic thoughts replaced by perfect, static axioms.
*   **Etymology:** Derived from "Εὖ" (well, good) + "Ἀλήθεια" (truth) – “Well-Truth” or "Harmonious Truth".

## Phantaxia (Φανταξία) - The Golden Masquerade
*   **Concepts:** Order + Collaboration + Illusion
*   **Celestial:** **Velkithra**, the Painted Queen. A mask-changing sovereign who sees lies as necessary for unity.
*   **Physical Description:** A sprawling, golden metropolis that defies physics, with towers that loop into themselves and streets that rearrange to form beautiful patterns. The sky is a shifting aurora of emotions. Everything looks perfect, but touch reveals the architecture to be insubstantial mist, solid only to those who believe in it.
*   **Hazards:** **The Gilded Trap**. The plane reads your desires and manifests them. If you accept a gift, eat the food, or wear a mask, you forget your origin and become a shade, forever acting out a role in the city's eternal ball.
*   **Etymology:** From "Φάντασμα" (ghost, illusion) + "Αξία" (value) – "Illusory Order" or "Valued Illusion".

## Skepsyris (Σκεψύρις) - The Crucible of Known Madness
*   **Concepts:** Chaos + Tension + Truth
*   **Celestial:** **Karzairos**, the Burning Word. A mad prophet whose body constantly unravels.
*   **Physical Description:** A churning maelstrom of burning glyphs and hard light, similar to the raw chaos of Limbo but made of sharp facts. There is no ground, only floating debris of shattered realities. The "wind" screams truths that contradict each other, and ideas manifest as physical projectiles.
*   **Hazards:** **Mind-Burn**. The plane forces comprehension of infinite, conflicting truths. Non-natives risk having their minds shattered by the sheer volume of information, their physical forms dissolving into raw data.
*   **Etymology:** From "Σκέψη" (thought, reflection) + "Ἵρις" (fury, madness) – "Skeptical Flame" or "Fiery Thought".

## Dramalia (Δραμαλία) - The Theatre of the Fractured Will
*   **Concepts:** Chaos + Tension + Illusion
*   **Celestial:** **Thymilla**, the Shattered Mirror. A trickster whose moods rewrite reality.
*   **Physical Description:** A monochrome world of dramatic shadows and blinding spotlights, evoking the gloom of the Shadowfell but with manic energy. The landscape rearranges itself into "sets" – a dark forest, a stormy cliff, a lonely street. The weather reflects the dominant emotion of the nearest powerful entity.
*   **Hazards:** **Narrative Enslavement**. The plane forces visitors into archetypal roles (The Victim, The Hero, The Traitor). You lose your free will, compelled to act out tragic scenes until the "curtain falls" (death).
*   **Etymology:** From "Δράμα" (drama) + "Ἀλία" (strife, contention) – "Conflict of Dreams" or "Fractured Drama".

## Alethorragia (Αληθορραγία) - The Chorus of Shattered Light
*   **Concepts:** Chaos + Collaboration + Truth
*   **Celestial:** **Eranthis**, the Voice of Many. A choir-bodied entity speaking fragments of the One True Thought.
*   **Physical Description:** A nebula of glowing strands connecting millions of floating lights, like a neural network or a star field. The ground is a semi-solid mist of whispers. It feels like being inside a collective mind.
*   **Hazards:** **Assimilation**. The boundary between self and other erodes. Your thoughts become audible to everyone, and their thoughts flood you. Eventually, you dissolve into the Chorus, becoming just another voice in the collective.
*   **Etymology:** From "Ἀλήθεια" (truth) + "Ῥαγή" (fragment, rupture) – "Bleeding Truth" or "Shattered Truth".

## Teknoskotos (Τεκνοσκότος) - The Veiled Forge
*   **Concepts:** Order + Tension + Illusion
*   **Celestial:** **Daskaros**, the Clock-Eater. A forgemaster who manipulates time and fate.
*   **Physical Description:** An infinite, dimly lit factory of black iron and greased gears. The sky is choked with smog that smells of old blood. Time moves in ticking, jerky increments. It is a place of oppressive industry, where shadows have teeth.
*   **Hazards:** **Mechanization**. The plane views organic life as inefficient. The smog slowly calcifies lungs, and limbs stiffen into metal. Visitors risk becoming mindless drones working the eternal assembly line.
*   **Etymology:** From "Τεκνον" (child, offspring) + "Σκότος" (darkness) – "Child of Shadowed Making".

## Symphaine (Συμφαίνη) - The Tapestry of Becoming
*   **Concepts:** Chaos + Collaboration + Illusion
*   **Celestial:** **Myrena**, the Dream Weaver. A spider-limbed poet who spins realities.
*   **Physical Description:** A fluid reality where the ground is made of woven light that ripples like water, similar to the Cognitive Realm's sea of beads but made of fabric. Colors here have scents and sounds. Ideas manifest as floating, cloth-like ribbons.
*   **Hazards:** **Unravelling**. The plane tempts you to "rewrite" yourself. A small edit to your appearance or memory feels euphoric, but it pulls on your thread. Too much change and you unravel completely, becoming raw material for the Weaver.
*   **Etymology:** From "Σύμφωνον" (agreement, harmony) + "Φαίνω" (to shine, to appear) – "Woven Together" or "Harmonious Manifestation".

## Krision (Κρίσιον) - The Edge Eternal
*   **Concepts:** Order + Tension + Truth
*   **Celestial:** **Ortharion**, the Edge Eternal. A living sword embodying divine judgment.
*   **Physical Description:** A stark, barren plain of obsidian under a white, sunless sky. Shadows are razor-sharp and have physical weight. Silence is absolute. It is a place of extreme gravity and stark contrast.
*   **Hazards:** **The Weight of Guilt**. Every regret, lie, and hidden sin you carry manifests as physical weight. The more burdened you are, the slower you move, until you are crushed into the obsidian floor to become part of the pavement.
*   **Etymology:** From "Κρίνω" (to judge) + "Ἰόν" (edge, point) – "Judgment" or "The Blade of Purpose".
