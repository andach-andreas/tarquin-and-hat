# Polyphotons

## Appearance and Characteristics

Polyphotons are small floating motes of light that can float over two living creatures and forcibly link their minds. Intelligent creatures can, if they wish, try to resist this connection, but it can be hard. 

Very skilled casters and magic users can overpower one or two polyphotons and force them to do their bidding - effectively binding their minds, rather than having their own mind bound. To do so with more than one takes exceptional mastery of your own mind and the arcane field. 

When two (or more) people are linked, their minds slowly merge into one and they take decisions as one, though physically act independently.

While they cannot be easily resisted, they can be easily summoned, and this can be exploited by intelligent creatures to get work done. The connection fades slowly over time, unless the mesh becomes too large, at which point only a powerful arcane caster will be able to undo the effects. 

## Effect on Society

On a world where a small number of polyphotons have come through, the population will generally regard them as part of life - sometimes useful, sometimes annoying. They will be used where needed in the legal system to prove truth, or to improve safety on a worksite by having two workers linked. 

Over time, individuals will grow used to the mind-altering effects, and everyone will gain some ability to break the link, but powerful arcane casters will still be needed for when the mesh grows (usually accidently) to more than 20 or so people. 

In some instances, the mesh will grow too large for the planets arcane casters to handle, and the individuals will have to be killed to prevent the spread.
