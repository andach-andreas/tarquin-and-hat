# Dreamweavers

## Appearance and Characteristics

A dreamweaver is a small ethereal, spider-like creature. They have a psychedelic effect on individuals and their presence induces a light "microdosing"-like state. Individuals become more creative, emotional and able to make connections between concepts more than before. 

Those with exceptional control of arcana and their own mind can use this to their advantage, unlocking the ability to cast more powerful spells, think quicker, and even read minds. The symbiosis is dangerous though, and can cause madness over time, multiple personalities and altered memories. 

## Effect on Society

Where dreamweavers are rare, they are highly prized by society, and kept to allow inventors and artists limited time at the heightened state. Fighters can use them to gain a significant edge in combat, and a skilled user of dreamweavers can fight at superhuman levels. 

On planets infested by them, the population cannot resist their influence and society degenerates into chaos quickly.
