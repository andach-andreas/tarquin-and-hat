# Chronarch Sentinels

## Appearance and Characteristics

The Coronarch Sentinels (often just 'Sentinels') are metallic humanoid creatures, orignating from Teknoskotos, the Veiled Forge. 

They appear rarely, but are highly dangerous. They enforce efficiency, bureaucracy and order wherever they are. They are not violent, but can transform a planet into a soulless world of technology.

Individually, the humanoid creatures are tall and incredibly strong, and would be able to lift a car easily. They do not express any emotion or effort. 

## Effect on Society

They avoid all forms of art, drama and entertainment, and have a calming effect on individuals. 

In worlds where the link is weak, a handful of sentinels can have a beneficial effect on engineering and technological advances, and individuals who spend lots of time with them will become scientific savants at the loss some of their humanity. 

Where the sentinels amass in large numbers, entire planets become devoid of emotion and opinion, and the individuals eventually lose all thought entirely. They farm food in giant hydroponic farms, transfer it to each other using automated robotic systems, and sit or lie in silence, exercising as required to keep muscle structure strong and fit.
