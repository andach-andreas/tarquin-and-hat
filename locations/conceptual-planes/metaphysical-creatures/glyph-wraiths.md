# Glyph Wraiths

## Appearance and Characteristics

Glyph Wraiths (typically just 'Wraiths') are terrifying contracictions made of swirling red mist and hard-light 'bodies'. 

They whisper truths and cause nightmares in order to spread chaos, but never lie. Typically they infest seats of high power and leaders to turn them against each other. 

## Integration into Society

Wraths cannot integrate into society. If they arrive at a planet in large numbers, they sow chaos, terror and societal breakdown and typically require intervention from groups in Pangenia to extract and close portals so that they do not come back. 

On occasion where this support from multiplanar entities and groups cannot be provided quick enough they typically result in war, famine and desolation.
