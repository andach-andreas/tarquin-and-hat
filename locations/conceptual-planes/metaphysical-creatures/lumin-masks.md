# Lumin Masks

## Appearance and Characteristics

Lumin Masks are native to Phantaxia, The Golden Masquerade. They appear as small grecien-style 'happy/sad' stage masks, able to take many different faces, but never changing when looked at. 

They float through the air and become real only when believed in. 

They do not have a direct individualistic will like intelligent creatures, but act en masse to mirror the large-scale emotions of individuals. 

Where they mass, they seek to encourage societal unity and will happily encourage lies and falsehoods to ensure this happens. 

## Integration into Societies

Lumin Masks need to mass in great numbers to have an effect on society, mostly being seen as an interesting or embarassing reflection of an individual's mood, particular in romantic contexts. 

Where they have gathered in greater numbers, they can be used as some sort of crude lie-detection mechanism, but will lie themselves to protect societies.
