# Biotic Magic (The School of Creation)

**Source:** The Primordial Creation.
**Core Concept:** Making. The generation of substance, life, and form from potential.

## Mechanics
*   **Catalysis:** Mortals cannot create from true nothingness. They need a "seed" or catalyst to expand upon.
*   **Vital Exchange:** Creation requires energy. The caster must often sacrifice their own life force or drain the surrounding environment to fuel the spell.

## Applications
*   **True Healing:** Regenerating lost limbs or organs (creating new tissue), unlike Divine healing which restores the "state" of the body.
*   **Growth:** Instantly growing plants from seeds to maturity.
*   **Transmutation:** Changing one form of matter into another (e.g., lead to gold, stone to bread).
*   **Animation:** Giving temporary "life" or movement to inanimate objects (golems).

## Limitations
*   **Physical Cost:** High-level spells can age the caster or leave them physically withered.
*   **Environmental Toll:** Large-scale creation can leave the surrounding area barren and sterile.
*   **The Taboo:** Creating true, sentient life (a soul) is forbidden and likely impossible for mortals, resulting in soulless abominations.
