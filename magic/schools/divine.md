# Divine Magic (The School of Being)

**Source:** The Primordial Being (via Ascended Gods).
**Core Concept:** Embodiment. Channeling the power of a being who perfectly personifies a concept.

## Mechanics
*   **Ascension:** A mortal can ascend to godhood by embodying a concept (e.g., Leadership, Nature, War) so perfectly that they become a living source of that concept's power.
*   **Granting:** Gods cannot be everywhere. They grant "seeds" of their power to devout followers. The follower acts as a conduit.
*   **Faith:** The strength of the magic depends on the follower's alignment with the God's domain and their conviction.

## Applications
*   **Healing:** The most common form. Restoring a body to its "ideal" state of being.
*   **Buffs/Debuffs:** Enhancing allies with the God's attributes (e.g., Strength, Courage) or weakening enemies.
*   **Miracles:** Rare, high-level interventions that defy natural laws (e.g., changing the weather, resurrection).

## Micah's Domain (Nature/Protection)
*   **Source:** Micah, the Druid God (currently a Hat).
*   **Focus:** Healing, plant growth, animal communication, and environmental protection.
*   **Unique Trait:** Since Micah is currently an object, his "granting" is passive. His power leaks into the world, fueling the ecosystem and allowing druids to tap into it without direct prayer.
