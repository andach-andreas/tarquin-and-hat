# Veridic Magic (The School of Memory)

**Source:** The Primordial Memory.
**Core Concept:** Truth. The absolute recall and understanding of reality as it was and is.

## Mechanics
*   **Passivity:** The caster must quiet their own Will and ego to become a vessel for the universe's memory.
*   **The Record:** Accessing the "Akashic Record" or the universe's perfect memory of all events.

## Applications
*   **Mnemonic (Recall):**
    *   **Psychometry:** Seeing the past of an object by touching it.
    *   **Acausal Perception:** Viewing events in a location's past.
*   **Veridic (Truth-Seeing):**
    *   **Truth Sense:** Instantly knowing if a statement is true or false.
    *   **Unveiling:** Shattering illusions by revealing the underlying reality.
*   **Narrative (Weaving):**
    *   **Story Empowerment:** Reinforcing a "true story" to manifest its power in the present (e.g., recounting a hero's legend to temporarily gain their strength).
    *   **Binding:** Strengthening seals or contracts by affirming their original "truth."

## Limitations
*   **Rarity:** Almost unknown.
*   **Mental Cost:** The human mind filters reality for a reason. Seeing absolute truth can lead to madness or catatonia.
*   **Cannot Change:** It cannot alter the past, only reveal it or draw power from it.
