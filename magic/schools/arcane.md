# Arcane Magic (The School of Will)

**Source:** The Primordial Will.
**Core Concept:** Agency. The imposition of the caster's will upon the physical laws of the universe.

## Mechanics
*   **The Field:** The Arcane Field permeates all of spacetime. It is a fundamental force, like gravity or electromagnetism.
*   **Casting:** A caster uses their own willpower, focused through mental discipline and often somatic/verbal components, to manipulate this field.
*   **Crystals:** The field can be condensed into a solid state—purple crystals. These act as batteries, allowing casters to store energy for later use or to power machines.

## Applications
*   **Evocation:** Manipulating energy (fire, lightning, force).
*   **Telekinesis:** Moving matter with the mind.
*   **Abjuration:** Creating shields and barriers by hardening the field.
*   **Utility:** Lighting, heating, cooling, and basic construction.

## Limitations
*   **Mental Strain:** Casting requires intense focus. Overexertion can lead to "Arcane Burnout," a state of mental and physical exhaustion.
*   **Conservation of Energy:** You cannot create energy from nothing; you are manipulating what is there.
