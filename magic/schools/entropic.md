# Entropic Magic (The School of Destruction)

**Source:** The Primordial Destruction.
**Core Concept:** Unmaking. The acceleration of entropy and the reduction of existence to nothingness.

## Mechanics
*   **The Void Call:** The caster connects to the destructive force. It requires a mindset of cold, detached finality.
*   **Erasure:** The magic does not "explode" things; it deletes them. It removes the bonds that hold matter and energy together.

## Applications
*   **Combat:**
    *   **Disintegration:** Turning matter to dust.
    *   **Severance:** Cutting through magical shields or bonds by "ending" their continuity.
*   **Utility (Dangerous):**
    *   **Precision Demolition:** Removing obstacles without collateral damage.
    *   **Medical Erasure:** "Ending" a tumor, a poison, or an infection. High risk of erasing healthy tissue.
    *   **Information Deletion:** Permanently removing memories or magical signatures.
*   **Stasis (The Paradox):** Creating a field where the *process of decay* is ended, preserving an object perfectly.

## The "Good" Use (The Finality Project)
*   A faction might try to use this for "good" (e.g., erasing a plague).
*   **Risk:** The "Eschatological Mindset." Users become cold and utilitarian, seeing problems as things to be "deleted" rather than solved.

## Limitations
*   **Cannot Create:** It can only take away.
*   **Corruption:** Prolonged use erodes the user's empathy and connection to life.
